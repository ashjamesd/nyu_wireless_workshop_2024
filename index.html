Visual Communications for the 2024 NYU Wireless Workshop

This project brought together three connected pieces of event communication: a save-the-date flyer, a printed workshop brochure, and on-site photography coverage. Each element supported a different stage of the attendee experience, from early event awareness to on-site navigation and post-event documentation.

Save-the-Date Flyer Design

The save-the-date flyer was created to give affiliates a quick overview of the workshop while building interest around the event. It highlighted the three main sessions, followed the NYU Wireless style guide, and included the four workshop organizers along with contact channels for the research center.

Workshop Brochure and On-Site Display Materials

The workshop brochure was designed as a practical guide attendees could carry throughout the event. It included the two-day schedule, speaker biographies, and affiliate information in a 9 x 12 trifold format.

One of the main challenges was fitting a large amount of content into a compact printed piece while maintaining readability. Careful attention was paid to typography, spacing, and hierarchy so the brochure could remain useful without feeling crowded.

The visual language of the brochure was also extended to digital graphics shown on monitors throughout the event space, helping create a cohesive identity across print and environmental display.

A strong sequence for this section is to first show the brochure itself, then show it in use at the workshop, and finally show how the design language was extended into digital event displays.

Event Photography Coverage

The final part of the project was photography coverage of the workshop itself. This documentation captured the atmosphere of the event, speaker presentations, attendee engagement, and the broader experience across the two-day program.

In addition to documenting the event, the photography provided material for recap communications, future promotions, and archival use.
